Welcome to the IPAI Group at the Dalian University, led by Associate Prof. Yuan Lin and Kan Xu.

Our research interests include Large Language Models, Information Retrieval and LLMs-based Multi-agents system for Intellectual Property and its application.

We primarily focus on research training for undergraduate students, welcoming participants from various disciplines.
