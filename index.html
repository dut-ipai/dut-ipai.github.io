About Us

Welcome to the IPAI Group at the Dalian University of Technology, led by Associate Prof. Yuan Lin and Kan Xu from DUTIR.

Our group is also led by Qiyao Wang, a Ph.D. student at CAS-SIAT.

We primarily focus on research training for undergraduate students, welcoming participants from diverse majors.

Research Interests

  • Large Language Models
  • Information Retrieval
  • LLMs-based Multi-Agents System
  • LLMs for Intellectual Property (mainly for Patents)
